Funding


Unfortunately UK Job Search cannot directly finance its users, however, there are resources available externally that we recommend here. these are just links; we have no affiliation with any other institutions and as such, we cannot offer support regarding their services. Regardless, if you are a UK citizen and in need of financial aid, this is a great place to start.

External Resources

  • The Scholarship Hub
  • https://www.thescholarshiphub.org.uk

  • "The Scholarship Hub was launched in early 2013 and is a social enterprise, which means we are committed to reinvesting at least 50% of any profits back into developing new scholarships."

  • TURN2US
  • https://www.turn2us.org.uk

  • "TURN2US helps people in financial need gain access to welfare benefits, charitable grants and other financial help - online, by phone and face to face through our partner organisations."

  • GOV UK
  • https://www.gov.uk/browse/education/student-finance
  • "You may be able to borrow money to help pay for university or college tuition fees and to help with living costs. you might get extra money on top of this, for example if you're on a low income, are disabled or have children."
